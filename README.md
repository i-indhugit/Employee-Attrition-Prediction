# Employee Attrition Prediction using Machine Learning

## 📌 Project Overview

Employee attrition is a major challenge for organizations because replacing employees increases recruitment, training, and productivity costs. This project uses Machine Learning to predict whether an employee is likely to leave the company based on various HR-related factors.

The project also provides business insights that can help HR teams improve employee retention and make better workforce decisions.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

- Analyze employee attrition data
- Clean and preprocess HR data
- Explore factors affecting employee attrition
- Build multiple Machine Learning models
- Compare model performance
- Identify the most important factors influencing attrition
- Provide HR recommendations
- Predict whether a new employee is likely to leave

---

## 📂 Dataset

Dataset: IBM HR Analytics Employee Attrition Dataset

Source:
https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/pavansubhasht/ibm-hr-analytics-attrition-dataset

Rows: 1470

Features: 35

Target Variable:
- Attrition (Yes / No)

## 💡 Key Business Insights

- Employees working overtime are more likely to leave.
- Lower job satisfaction increases attrition risk.
- Employees with fewer years at the company are more likely to resign.
- Salary alone does not explain attrition.
- Work-life balance has a strong influence on employee retention.

---

## ✅ HR Recommendations

- Reduce excessive overtime.
- Improve employee engagement programs.
- Conduct regular employee feedback sessions.
- Focus on retaining employees during their early years.
- Improve work-life balance initiatives.
